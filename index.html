Who I am

I try to tell stories through my songs

I am a singer, songwriter and composer from India (Ludhiana, Punjab). I've been singing since I was a kid but I got into music properly when I was 16, this was the time when I started learning the guitar.

It all started with a band in my school. When I saw the guitarist performing, I immediately knew this was something I wanted to do in the future and gladly since then, there has been no turning back.

In each one of my songs, I try to tell a story. I try that the songs make you fall in love, make you long for something that you haven't ever felt. I hope you feel things from my perspective.
